Discover Salar de Uyuni

The massive expanse of salt formed after a prehistoric lake dried up. Found among the Andes in Bolivia, Salar de Uyuni covers more than a whopping 4,000 square miles.Bright salt and rock structures spread across the landscape. Wildlife is rarely seen at the salt flat, but pink flamingos commonly visit the area.
